Canter College

If you are a student of our school, you can log in to view your schedule







Canter College offers one to nine weeks of fully embedded learning courses for students who wish to experience Australian education, from kindergarten to 12th grade. Canter College offers short-term study abroad programs to allow international and Australian students to experience the Australian way of life together in the beautiful Queensland. International students can use visitor visas to experience up to ten weeks of study and life at Canter College. Students learn education courses in Queensland through immersive courses, and the college provides English language support if needed. Host families will pick up and drop off students to school during these short-term experiences. Cost: $900 AUD per week (seven days). A deposit of $800 Australian dollars is required and can be returned at the end of the course if there is no damage. Including: tuition fees, textbooks, boarding, airport transfers, school transfers, all meals and snacks, Australian experience tours, farewell barbecue parties. The cost does not include: airfare, personal expenses, and visa fees for coming to Australia.
