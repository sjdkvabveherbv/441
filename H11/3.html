International students

Welcome to browse our webpage. When browsing our website, you will find that we are a group of like-minded educators. Our common dream is to enable all students to unleash their maximum potential in academic, quality, social, and cultural aspects in an motivational learning environment. We not only have superior indoor and outdoor hardware facilities, but we also respect Christian values, encourage active brainstorming, develop creative thinking, and embrace different ideas. At Canterbury School, we cultivate confident young people with high self-esteem and self-discipline. I believe Canterbury School is the ideal choice for international students to study in Australia. Our international students are arranged to stay with local English speaking families. Our host family ensures that every student can experience all aspects of Australian life, from dining, going out to conversations. Students will gain a deeper understanding of our culture and be able to practice English in a more relaxed family atmosphere. We require host families to achieve the following:
-Provide students with as much care and support as possible, treating them as members of their own family;
-Provide comfortable and clean single rooms for each student. The room must have good ventilation, sufficient lighting, and be suitable for learning. The room must have a comfortable bed and clean sheets, wardrobe, desk, and chairs. The room should be considered as the student's private space;
-Provide nutritious daily meals and snacks;
-The distance from the school cannot be too far.
We require host families to keep the host coordinator informed of any changes in their living environment at all times.
Our school provides the following services for international students at no charge
-ESL experts on campus can also assist students in completing homework, homework, and exam preparation outside of class through email.
-Set up a learning area in the English center on campus, equipped with learning resources, refrigerators and microwaves for daily use by students
-English small class teaching to further cultivate students' English skills such as listening, speaking, reading, and writing
-Offering special courses as a supplement to all subjects
-All classrooms are equipped with personal laptops
-The study of English literature adopts professional English textbooks
-Provide additional tutoring for all subjects during lunchtime and after class
-Electronic dictionaries can be used during exams, additional assistance from teachers can be obtained, and time can be extended to improve exam results
-Organize meals every semester to give students the opportunity to communicate and interact with other classmates and teachers
-The number of international students is not large, so they can communicate with Australian students anytime during class and lunch time
-Our school values communication between teachers and parents, and delivers reports to parents in the first, second, and fourth semesters
-10. 11th and 12th grade students can participate in internships in Australian companies
-University courses are offered on campus, and collaborative courses are offered with Griffith University, Queensland, and South Queensland University. 12th grade students can take university courses
